Please write a sokoban game. 
Where there are a player, crates, holes and walls. 
The player can move around in a 2d grid map. 
When player collide wall, player will step back. 
When player hits the crate, crate will bounce forward.  
When crate hit hole, both hole and crate will disappear. 
When crate hit the wall, crate will step back as well. 
When all holes disappear, game will win. 
There is no lose condition.

Please write a arcade zelda-like game. 
Where there are a PlayerWithoutKey, a PlayerWithKey, enemies, walls, one key and one door. 
The PlayerWithoutKey and PlayerWithKey can move around in a 2d grid map. 
When PlayerWithoutKey and PlayerWithKey collide the wall, the PlayerWithoutKey and PlayerWithKey will step back. 
When PlayerWithoutKey hits the key, the PlayerWithoutKey will transformTo PlayerWithKey.
When PlayerWithoutKey and PlayerWithKey hits the enemies, PlayerWithoutKey and PlayerWithKey will be killed.  
When PlayerWithKey hits door, door will disappear. 
When enemies hit wall, enemies will step back.
When number of player equals to zero, game will lose.
When number of door equals to zero, game will win. 
Enemies will wonder randomly.
